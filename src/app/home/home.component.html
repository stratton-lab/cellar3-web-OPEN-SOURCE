Top Features
Differential Expression
Identify genes with varying expression levels across single cells, shedding light on cellular diversity and function. Visualize these differences with Volcano, Violin, Dot and UMAP plots.
Functional Analysis
Unveil the biological functions and pathways associated with the differentially expressed genes. Differential Expression will be performed first.
Pathway Analysis
Display differentially expressed genes on KEGG pathway maps. This visualization highlights gene interactions and biological pathways, providing insights into cellular diversity and function.
Pseudotime Analysis
Track the progression of cells through different stages of development or differentiation. This feature visualizes dynamic gene expression changes along the pseudotime trajectory, offering insights into temporal cellular transitions and lineage decisions.
Spatial
Explore the intricate spatial organization of individual cells within their native tissue or organism. The Spatial feature allows you to visualize each cell in its precise location, offering insights into the spatial relationships, interactions, and patterns within complex biological structures.
Cell-Cell Interactions
Visualize and analyze communication between different cell types. This feature highlights receptor-ligand interactions, revealing crucial intercellular signaling pathways and providing insights into cell coordination and function within the tissue.
Open & Reproducible Science
Receipt
To improve reproducibility, a “receipt” is available, listing backend libraries used for the processing, along with their versions and supplied parameters.
Real-Time Analysis
SingloCell enhances the reusability of existing datasets by enabling easy modification and real-time analysis, facilitating new discoveries.
Data Submission
A streamlined process for contributing datasets. Transparent data sharing and reproducibility, fostering collaboration and advancing open science initiatives.
